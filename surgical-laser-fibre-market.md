<p><ul><li>Reusable Laser Fibers</li><li>Disposable Laser Fibers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surgical laser fiber market is divided into two main types: reusable and disposable laser fibers. Reusable laser fibers are designed for multiple uses, offering cost-effectiveness and reduced waste, but require thorough sterilization after each use. In contrast, disposable laser fibers are single-use products that eliminate the risk of cross-contamination and save on sterilization costs, making them more convenient in high-volume surgical settings. Both types play critical roles in various surgical procedures, enhancing precision and patient outcomes.</p></p>
