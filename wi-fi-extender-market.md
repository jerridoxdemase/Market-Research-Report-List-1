<p><strong>The Wi-Fi Extender Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wi-Fi Range Extender</li><li>Wireless AP Router</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wi-Fi extender market primarily comprises two types: Wi-Fi range extenders and wireless access point (AP) routers. Wi-Fi range extenders amplify existing signals to eliminate dead zones and enhance connectivity in larger spaces. They work by receiving the original signal and rebroadcasting it. Wireless AP routers, on the other hand, create new Wi-Fi networks by connecting to a wired network, offering stronger signals and greater coverage. Both serve to improve network performance and accessibility in homes and businesses.</p></p>
