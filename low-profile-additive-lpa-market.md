<p><p>Low Profile Additive (LPA) is utilized in various composite manufacturing processes to enhance surface quality and reduce shrinkage. In injection and compression molding, LPA improves mold aesthetics and dimensional stability. In pultrusion, it aids in achieving smooth finishes while maintaining structural integrity. Resin Transfer Molding (RTM) benefits from LPA by providing optimal flow characteristics and reduced air entrapment. Additionally, in hand lay-up and spray-up applications, LPA enhances the visual appearance and reduces laminate thickness, ensuring efficient production with superior surface properties.</p></p>
