<p><p>The hydraulic mulch additives market is categorized into two main types: acidic and alkaline. Acidic additives help lower soil pH, enhancing nutrient availability for certain plants, particularly in alkaline soils. Conversely, alkaline additives increase soil pH, making them suitable for acidic soils where a neutral to slightly alkaline environment is beneficial. Both types play a crucial role in improving soil health, moisture retention, and vegetation establishment, ultimately aiding in erosion control and sustainable agriculture practices.</p></p>
