<p><strong>The Wireless Charging Integrated Circuits ICs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transmitter IC</li><li>Receiver IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless charging integrated circuits (ICs) market consists of transmitter and receiver ICs. Transmitter ICs are responsible for generating and controlling the electromagnetic field that transfers energy to the receiver. They manage power delivery and communication protocols to ensure efficient charging. Receiver ICs, on the other hand, are designed to capture the energy from the transmitter and convert it into usable power for the device. Both types are essential for enabling seamless wireless charging in consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial applications.</p></p>
