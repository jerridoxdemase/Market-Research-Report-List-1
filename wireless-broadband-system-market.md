<p><p>The wireless broadband system market comprises three main types: Fixed Wireless Broadband, Mobile Wireless Broadband, and Satellite Wireless Broadband. Fixed Wireless Broadband utilizes radio signals to provide internet access to stationary locations, often serving rural areas where traditional infrastructure is limited. Mobile Wireless Broadband offers internet connectivity on-the-go through cellular networks, catering to smartphones and portable devices. Satellite Wireless Broadband relies on satellite technology to deliver internet services globally, making it ideal for remote locations where terrestrial options are unavailable.</p></p>
