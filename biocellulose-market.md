<p><ul><li>Static Method</li><li>Dynamic Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biocellulose market can be categorized into two types based on production methods: static and dynamic. The static method involves traditional fermentation techniques where cellulose-producing microorganisms are cultivated under controlled conditions, resulting in a stable, high-purity product. In contrast, the dynamic method employs more advanced processes, such as stirred-tank bioreactors, which enhance efficiency and yield by providing constant mixing and nutrient availability. Both methods are essential for meeting the growing demand for biocellulose in industries like healthcare, food, and cosmetics.</p></p>
