<p><ul><li>Wet Process LMO</li><li>Dry Process LMO</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LMO (Lithium Manganese Oxide) battery materials market is categorized into two primary types: wet process LMO and dry process LMO. The wet process involves the chemical synthesis of LMO using liquid solutions, resulting in high purity and uniformity, making it suitable for high-performance batteries. In contrast, the dry process utilizes solid-state synthesis, which is generally more cost-effective and scalable but may yield lower purity levels. Both methods cater to the growing demand for efficient and sustainable energy storage solutions.</p></p>
