Immunoassays leverage specific antibodies for quantifying substances, ensuring high sensitivity and specificity, often applicable in disease diagnosis and drug testing. Dipstick tests, simple and portable, utilize absorbent strips to provide qualitative results for substances such as glucose or proteins in urine. Together, they enhance accessible health monitoring and early diagnosis.</p></p>
