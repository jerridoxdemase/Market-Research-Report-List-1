<p><ul><li>Monocomponent</li><li>Bi-component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solvent-free polyurethane adhesive market is primarily segmented into monocomponent and bi-component types. Monocomponent adhesives are ready-to-use formulations that cure upon exposure to moisture in the air, offering convenience and ease of application. In contrast, bi-component adhesives require mixing two components—resin and hardener—prior to use, allowing for customizable properties and enhanced performance. Both types are favored for their environmental benefits and high bonding strength, making them suitable for various industrial applications, including woodworking, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
