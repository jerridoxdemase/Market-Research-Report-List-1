<p><p>Surface mount fuses are categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type II fuses, on the other hand, are optimized for lower power applications, offering faster response times to overcurrents and maintaining compact designs. Both types are integral in protecting electronic devices from damage while ensuring reliability in various circuit configurations.</p></p>
