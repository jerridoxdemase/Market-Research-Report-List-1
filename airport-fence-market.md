<p><p>The airport fence market comprises three main types: welded, bar, and woven. Welded type fences provide durability and high-security levels, typically using rigid panels. The bar type features vertical steel bars spaced apart, offering visibility and strength, making it suitable for perimeter protection. Woven type fences consist of interlinked wires, providing flexibility and ease of installation. Each type caters to different security needs and aesthetic preferences, ensuring effective protection for airport perimeters while enhancing overall safety and surveillance.</p></p>
