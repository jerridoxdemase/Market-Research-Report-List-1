<p><p>The RFMicrowave Inductors market includes wire wound, film, and multilayer types. Wire wound inductors consist of coils wrapped around a core, offering high performance at higher inductance values. Film inductors utilize thin layers of conductive materials, ideal for low-frequency applications with minimal size and weight. Multilayer inductors stack multiple layers of magnetic materials to achieve compact designs and improved inductance, making them suitable for high-frequency applications in modern electronics, including RF devices and wireless communication systems.</p></p>
